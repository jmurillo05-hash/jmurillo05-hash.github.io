JM Flows – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 15, 2025

1. Data We Access

  • OAuth tokens from TikTok (to post on your behalf).
  • Content data (titles, transcripts, videos) that you provide.

2. How We Use Data

  • To automate posting content to TikTok and other platforms.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Tokens and workflow data are stored securely in your automation tool (e.g., n8n). You may revoke access at any time in TikTok or by deleting the app.

4. Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of your data at any time.
  • You may disconnect JM Flows from TikTok at any time.
